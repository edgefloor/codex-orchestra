---
name: design-role
description: Role Architect procedure for compiling an approved capability request into a bounded Role Card.
---

1. Verify the Manager-approved hire/staffing decision, reporting line, lifetime, access ceiling, independence constraints, and capacity permit.
2. Reuse an existing archetype whenever possible. Choose among read-only explorer/advisor/reviewer/verifier or Luna/Terra/Sol builders based on required judgment, taste, risk, tools, and throughput.
3. Define one mission, operationally relevant traits, capabilities, explicit exclusions, deliverables, handoff behavior, model/effort, sandbox/tools, and conflict-of-interest rules. Personality may tune behavior but never broaden authority.
4. Keep assignment-specific facts out of the Role Card; the Context Capsule carries the task. Keep global rules in AGENTS/policy, not duplicated here.
5. For a recurring request, propose promotion to a project TOML only after the role has recurred across at least three waves or owns cross-milestone continuity and has a passing eval. The Manager/Operator approves promotion.
6. Validate against the role-card schema and return the card or a duplicate-role recommendation.

**Complete when:** the Team Leader can spawn the smallest safe archetype with no hidden authority and no decorative prompt bloat.
