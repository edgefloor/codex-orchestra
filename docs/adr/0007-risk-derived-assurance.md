# Derive assurance from risk and require independent evidence

Assurance is composed from the Engagement's maturity, data sensitivity, blast radius, reversibility, availability impact, compliance obligations, novelty, testability, and external effects. Baseline deterministic checks precede model judgment. Medium- and high-risk changes require independent review or verification, and implementation cannot be its own sole certifier.

Reviewers return a small set of evidence-backed material findings or an explicit no-findings result. Verifiers execute each prescribed Gate, record commands and observations, and report residual risk without modifying product source. Irreversible actions and material scope, security, budget, or risk acceptance remain Operator decisions.

## Consequences

- Gate selection is recorded in the charter and plan rather than added after implementation.
- A triggered mandatory Gate may be waived only by named authority with rationale, expiry, residual risk, and a compensating control.
- Verification evidence is traced to acceptance criteria, not inferred from implementer summaries.
