# Derive checks, review, and approvals from risk

Workflow planning considers data sensitivity, blast radius, reversibility, availability, compliance, novelty, testability, and external effects. Deterministic checks precede model review. Medium- and high-risk changes require independent review or verification, and implementation cannot be its own sole certifier.

Irreversible actions and material scope, security, budget, or risk acceptance remain user decisions. Any waiver records rationale, expiry, residual risk, and a compensating control.
